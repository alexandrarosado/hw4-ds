Summer in SF

Sister Visit

My older sister came to visit!! We enjoyed Google's family day in Mountain View and explored the city together as we have never been to the west coast!

Foodie

This summer I became quite the foodie in San Fran. I enjoyed lots of great Ramen, Dim Sum, Sushi, and the BEST fruit from Chinatown.

Friends

I made SO many memories with my cohort and now lifelong friends. From being traumatized at Yosemite (long story) to city events, there was never a dull moment!

Views

Everyday I am so incredibly thankful to be able to enjoy the views I do. I hiked through Yosemite, Muir Woods, the Palace of Fine Arts, Golden Gate Park, and more.

Activities

This summer I tried lots of new activities. I attended puppy yoga, went to community dance socials, and will definitely miss watching seals off of pier 39.

Culture

Fun Fact!! Peru's Independence Day is July 28th, 1821. I was back home and attended the Peruvian parade in Patterson, NJ with my family.
